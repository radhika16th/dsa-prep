## Linked Lists

## Nodes connected by pointers (not contiguous memory)

## Types
- Singly Linked List
- Doubly Linked List

### Time Complexities
Access: O(1)

Search: O(1)

Insertion/Deletion: O(1)

## Common Patterns
- Fast & Slow Pointer  
- Reversal  
- Merging

## To use when:
- Frequent insertion and deletion
- Dynamic Size
- Tracking a series of states (undo/redo, back/forward)
- Managing ttracks that can be reordered or looped (music)
